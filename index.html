Who We Are

Bridging Academic Insight & Real-World Projects.

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We believe that effective risk management requires a synthesis of deep theoretical understanding and practical, hands-on experience. Our programs are designed to bridge this gap, creating a new generation of risk practitioners.

UK University Partnership

We leverage cutting-edge research from our academic partners to inform our training models and innovation lab.
